1️⃣ Problem / Solution (Kya Problem Solve Kar Rahe Ho)

Users ko clearly batao kya issue hai aur kaise aap solve kar rahe ho.

Example:

“Finding verified, affordable rooms in Mumbai can be stressful for students and professionals. Our platform makes it simple to search, compare, and contact landlords safely.”

2️⃣ Team Members (Kaun Kaun Hai)

Short intro of team members + roles.

Optional: Small profile pics ya icons.

Example:

Meet Our Team:
- Rupesh: Frontend & UI/UX
- Sarthak: Backend & Database
- Harsh: Product & Marketing

3️⃣ Achievements / Milestones (Trust Build Karne Ke Liye)

Agar kuch stats ya achievements hain, show karo:

Rooms listed: 500+

Happy tenants: 300+

Cities covered: Mumbai, Pune
